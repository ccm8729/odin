My career at a glance



Recently, I've been doing work as a business analyst - working for Teknor Apex, I was gathering requirements for modifications to an ERP called Infor M3, then putting those requirements into a specification for the purpose of guiding programmers as they develop this modification. Everything from how the modification should look, to how it should interact with the database, what test cases should pass vs. error, etc. I enjoyed this job greatly, and excelled at it - however, I decided to leave this job with the explicit intention of moving to Germany, to pursue a lifestyle more suited to my tastes.

In the past, my career had a mixed focus on supply chain management and QA testing/requirements development for new ERP implimentations across multiple business units of Manuli Hydraulics. My focus varied here, from how to forecast demand for new/existing businesses, to determining whether ERP functionality would fit the business process of the company.

I'm happy to provide contact information for any of the positions in my history - I excelled within each position, and my previous managers would all be happy to speak to you in detail regarding specific duties, competencies, skills, etc. And of course, you can find more details at the 'professional' link on the sidebar.
